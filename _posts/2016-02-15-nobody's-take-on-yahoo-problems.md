### Prelogue

It was Pre 2007 somwewhere, I heard 'yahoo is a media company' with a lot of media related products in its kitty. Yahoo! was hard fighting within as what it was. Terry Semel's short sightedness also let google walk away, when they were ready to sell for 1 Billion. 

### Year 2007 - 2008

Jerry Yang at helm started to build it back as a tech company. We were working hard to bring the search back to Yahoo! The investors, we kept hearing were not happy, and even before the search team could do somthing substantial, they were axed. I am talking of Tuoc Luong, Prabhakaran, Nam. All were so good, that Yahoo! could easily beat any search company today. However, Rome was not built in a day. It takes time and soon the team was disbanded.

### Years after

Nothing has changed, Yahoo! had been ever since searching for, what it wanted to do in this market from being a super portal. Marisa Meyer has been trying hard turning into a mobile centric company, but investors are pressing hard on her.

### My Understanding

Throughtout Yahoo! has been missing the train, in time and mostly because of some investors trying to make a quick buck. Technology company does not work like this. It has costed the investors and also this wonderful company. Yahoo! is/was known for its strong anti provacy norms and openness. It had great tech and great people. Some of the smartest people!
