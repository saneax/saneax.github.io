Colors and spaces
like fast moving river
changes rapidly. as the sun races
down the horizon, a prayer
hold on please, stop, you wish it freezes
the moment in my life I wish to hover
for a bit more, ceases



my thoughts embraces
as the sun goes down, casts forever
