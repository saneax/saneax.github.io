A horrendous crime done by insane men and a girl died brutally. We all hide our heads in shame! However, I am at loss of words at seeing

  • facebook updates with pictures of the girl, before and after and explicitly describing what happened.
  • news channels continuously debating and asking the public, 'what do you think about the incidence?'
  • news channel going after the village and talking to every person in the village.
  • political parties running a race to give goodies like naming roads, day etc etc..
I am no saint, and probably even worse judge of things happening. But it surely feels like this society raped her again and again for their narrow objectives. TV trp ratings, political mileage, 2 minutes of limelight in front of a crowd. 

RIP, probably this is a bad age.
