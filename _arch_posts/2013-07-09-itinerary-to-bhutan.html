Itinerary to Bhutan (ver 2 After HVK inputs, thank you Sir)



15 Dec to 19 Dec - 4 Days - travel from bangalore to Kolkata
19 Dec to 21 Dec - 3 Days with family


22 Dec - Day 1 - (start at 10PM 21 Dec from Kolkata, Night/Day drive - Kolkata - Dumka - Siliguri - WBTDC Jaldapara Resort) 800 Kms and stay over night.
23 Dec - Day 2 - (drive early to reach Phuntsoling by 8:30, take permit to Paro and Thimphu and drive to Paro) 198 kms
24 Dec - Day 3 - Drive to Thimphu(Get permits for other places, Make sure you get a Cultutal Ministry permit to visit inside Punakha Dzong) - Return to Paro - 124 kms
25 Dec - Day 4 - Paro - stay and relax
26 Dec - Day 5 - Trek to Taktsang (Tigers Nest) relax
27 Dec - Day 6 - Trek to Jele Dzong and Camp
28 Dec - Day 7 - Return to Paro
29 Dec - Day 8 - Paro - Thimphu - Punakha (stay at Punakha) 122 Kms
30 Dec - Day 9 - Punakha - Wangdue Phodrang, Bhutan - Gangtey Monastery, Trongsa 156 kms (rest at Trongsa)
01 Dec - Day 7 - Trongsa - jakar - Bumthang - 77 Kms
02 Dec - Day 8 - Bumthang - mongar 184 kms
03 Dec - Day 9 - mongar - Lhuntse trek (Festival * Lhuntse Tshechu)
04 Jan - Day 10 - Back to Mongar (relax)
05 Jan - Day 12 - Mongar - trashigang - Samdrup Jhonkar - Tezpur 465 kms (not sure If I can do that)
06 jan - Day 13 - Buffer
07 Jan - day 14 - Tezpur - Tawang 325 kms (pesses for Tawan/Ziroto be procured from kolkata AN office)
08 Jan - Day 15 - Rest
09 Jan - Day 16 - Tawang - Seppa 378 Kms
10 Jan - Day 17 - Seppa - ziro 288 kms
11 Jan - Day 18 - Ziro - Narayanapur - Tezpur 285 kms
12 jan - Day 19 - Tezpur - Siliguri - 544 kms (I am not sure if this is diable although)
13 Jan - Day 20 - Buffer
14 Jan - Day 21 - Siliguri Kolkata Via Dumka

15 Jan to 16 Jan - 5 Days with Family
16 Jan to 19 Jan - Drive back to Bangalore
